Our Story

Three decades of building a craft.

"Our road to becoming one of the leading Sri Lankan garment manufacturers for women was long and winding — but hard work and constant effort to meet the highest quality standards resulted in a wide variety of fashionable products now seen throughout the world."

30+
Years of experience manufacturing ladies wear

Over 30 years of experience made us experts in manufacturing ladies wear — particularly blouses, tops, skirts and dresses — and, more recently, leisurewear and activewear, all crafted from first-quality fabrics sourced from around the world.

Meeting the highest quality standards is the result of latest technology, right leadership from management, hardworking employees, and designers who train our workers and supervise the entire process from sample to delivery.
