Our Journey

From Sri Lanka
to the World

Our road to becoming one of the leading Sri Lankan garment manufacturers for Women was long and winding — but hardwork and constant effort to meet the highest quality standards resulted in a wide variety of fashionable products that can now be seen throughout the world.

Over 30 years of experience made us experts in manufacturing ladies wear, particularly blouse, top, skirt and dress categories. Lately the leisure wear and activewear — all made from first quality fabrics from around the world.
